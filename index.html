Colorado College

Rugby

Welcome to the official webpage of the Colorado College Rugby Football Club. Located in Colorado Springs, CCRFC was founded in 1967 with a current membership of over 80 undergraduate students and member of the Eastern Rocky Mountain Rugby Football Union.

We practice on campus 2 to 4 times a week and welcome all skill levels to our trainings. Please contact our current Captain or Coach from the information provided on the Contact page for practice details.
